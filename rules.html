RULES Rock beats Scissors (Rock crushes Scissors). Scissors beat Paper (Scissors cut Paper). Paper beats Rock (Paper covers Rock). If both players choose the same shape, the game is a tie and usually replayed. first one to reach 5 points win the game Example Scenarios: You choose Rock and bot chooses Scissors: you earn 1 point. You choose Paper and bot chooses Rock: you earn 1 point. You choose Scissors and bot chooses Paper: you earn 1 point. You choose Rock and bot chooses Rock: It's a tie; no point is gained for either the bot or you.
